About Us

Welcome to the College Poker Club, where everyone is invited to join the exciting world of poker!

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for students of all skill levels to enjoy and learn the game of poker. We aim to foster a sense of community, strategy, and fun among our members.

Club Goals

The goals of our club include:

  • Introducing beginners to the fundamentals of poker.
  • Providing a platform for advanced players to enhance their skills.
  • Promoting a friendly and educational atmosphere.

What to Expect

Whether you're a seasoned pro or a complete beginner, our club is open to all students. We provide poker sets and cards for our events. The club is designed to be both fun and educational, but it's important to note that we do not involve real money gambling in our activities.
