Website Evaluations

Amazon

An online retailer that sells many different products.

  • Contrast: Utilizes the same color scheme throughout where product images, prices, and buttons stand out distinctly against a neutral background.
  • Repetition: Uses consistent design elements throughout the site, specifically the search bar, nagivation menu, and logo.
  • Alignment: Elements are aligned neatly with a structured layout. The product listings are in a grid alignment.
  • Proximity: Related information such as product information and the "Add to Cart" button are placed in close proximity.

Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc.

An online catalog for an x-ray equipment company.

  • Contrast: Very little contrast with the dark neon background behind black and yellow links, making it hard to read.
  • Repetition: Each product page is pretty consistent in layout, however some of the color choices seem random and don't fit with the rest of the page.
  • Alignment: A lot of the product pages have sections of text that aren't aligned with one another.
  • Proximity: The proximity of elements isn't horrible and the spacing of elements seems conistent.
